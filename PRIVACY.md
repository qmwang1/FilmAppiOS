# Filmist Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Filmist is a film photography log app for tracking film stocks, rolls, cameras, development status, backups, and attached photos.

## Data Stored by the App

Filmist may store information that you enter into the app, including:

- Film stock details, such as brand, model, ISO, format, frame count, roll count, and expiry date
- Roll status history, such as unloaded, loaded, finished, in development, and developed
- Camera and lens information
- Dates related to loading, finishing, and processing rolls
- Photos that you choose to attach to developed rolls
- Backup files created by the app

## How Data Is Stored

Filmist stores your app data locally on your device.

If you choose to use iCloud backup, Filmist stores a backup of your film log and attached image files in the app's private iCloud container associated with your Apple ID.

If you choose to export a backup file, the exported file is saved only to the location you select, such as Files, iCloud Drive, AirDrop, or another storage provider.

## Data Sharing

Filmist does not sell your data.

Filmist does not use third-party advertising or third-party tracking.

Filmist does not send your film log, camera information, dates, photos, or backup files to the developer's server.

## Photos

Filmist only accesses photos that you choose to import into the app. Imported photos are copied into the app's local storage and may be included in backups that you create.

## iCloud

iCloud backup is optional. If enabled and used, backup data is stored through Apple's iCloud service in the app's private iCloud container. Your use of iCloud is also subject to Apple's privacy policy and iCloud terms.
